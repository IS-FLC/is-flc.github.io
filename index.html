Welcome to LEGGO

This is a web application that hopes to allow visitors as well as locals to discover Glasgow on a deeper level. It is designed to cater to locals and visitors whom are free to share their memories of their favourite hangout spots. They can denote waypoints in which they can write short description about their experiences. Users of the application can also view different routes. Users can also give their own feedback about their experience or suggest a better route. Based on the voting system, we can ensure quality comments and routes being on the top of the list.
